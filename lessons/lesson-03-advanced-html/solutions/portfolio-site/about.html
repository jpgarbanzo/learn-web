About Sarah Mitchell

Passionate developer, continuous learner, and problem-solving enthusiast

My Development Journey

My journey into web development began during my college years when I created my first website for a local nonprofit organization. What started as a simple volunteer project quickly became a passion that would shape my entire career path.

After graduating with a degree in Computer Science, I dove headfirst into the world of full-stack development. I've had the privilege of working with startups, established companies, and freelance clients, each experience teaching me something new about technology, user experience, and the power of well-crafted code.

What Drives Me

I'm motivated by the endless possibilities that code creates. Every project is a puzzle to solve, a user experience to improve, or a business problem to address through technology. I believe that great software isn't just functional—it's intuitive, accessible, and makes people's lives a little bit easier.
