  • Call +1 (234) 567-8900
  • Local Business

SMS Links

Links that open SMS/text messaging apps:

  • Send SMS to +1 (234) 567-8900
  • SMS with Pre-filled Message

Download Links

Rel Attribute (Relationship)

  • Secure External Link
  • Untrusted External Link
  • Next Page in Series
  • Previous Page in Series

Accessibility Best Practices

Good Link Text Examples:

  • Contact our customer service team
  • Download the annual report (PDF, 2.3MB)
  • Example.com (opens in new window)

Poor Link Text Examples (Avoid These):

  • Click here for more information
  • Click here to download
  • Visit our website at https://example.com
